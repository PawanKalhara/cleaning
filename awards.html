Accreditations

  • British Institute of Cleaning Science (BICSc)

    • BiSC - At Ritz Eco Facilities Management, we have BICSc accredited trained assessors who instruct our cleaning operatives to adhere to these high standards. We also maintain corporate BICSc membership, which you can confirm by clicking the logo on our website's home page. The top accredited cleaning companies should ideally possess BICSc accreditation - don't hesitate to ask them why they lack this if that's the case.

  • Alcumas Safe Contractor

    • "At Ritz Cleaners, we hold full Aclumas SafeContractor accreditation, which we view as essential for the best accredited cleaning companies. Aclumas SafeContractor enables professional cleaning firms to demonstrate their commitment to delivering best practices that go beyond mere health and safety. This accreditation provides businesses seeking a cleaning company or other professional trade with the peace of mind that their supply chain is secure and resilient, allowing contractors to showcase their adherence to a range of crucial criteria, including health and safety, environmental responsibility, modern slavery prevention, diversity, equality, workplace well-being, and financial stability, all under one certification."

  • Safety Management Advisory Services (SMAS)

    • With a presence in the industry since 2006, we have accumulated extensive expertise in the realm of health and safety, specializing in assisting businesses with their health and safety responsibilities. As an affirmation of our dedication to this cause, we make certain that every assessor undergoes SSIP Assessor Training and undergo independent SSIP Auditor evaluations. As a result, it is unsurprising that Ritz Cleaners has proudly maintained full SMAS accreditation for several years.

  • Contractors. Health and Safety, Assessment (CHAS)

    • Since 1997, CHAS has established itself as a reliable provider of health and safety (H&S) assessments. They are at the forefront of delivering compliance and risk management services, assisting contractors, including those in commercial cleaning, as well as clients (buyers) in showcasing their adherence to their acclaimed supply chain and risk management services. As co-founders and pioneers of the Safety Schemes in Procurement (SSIP) and the Common Assessment Standard, CHAS leads the industry by setting high compliance standards that elevate supply chain risk management practices nationwide. Our mission is clear: to enhance workplace safety for businesses, employees, and the general public. When in search of accredited cleaning companies, it's crucial to prioritize both SMAS and CHAS accreditations. Once again, Ritz Cleaners proudly holds full CHAS accreditation, a recognition we've maintained for several years.

  • ISO 9001

    • Ritz manages and upholds the ISO 9001 quality management system. Our accreditation guarantees that we consistently uphold a dedicated commitment to delivering quality. Within this framework, we regularly assess and enhance our management and company processes to ensure ongoing improvement.

  • ISO 14001

    • We operate and uphold the ISO 14001 environmental management system. Our accreditation guarantees that, as a company, we effectively manage and control various environmental aspects under our direct influence. Continuous review and improvement of our management and company processes are integral to this system, as affirmed by our compliance with the Energy Savings Opportunity Scheme (ESOS). Moreover, we not only showcase ongoing adherence to our company's environmental policy but also ensure compliance with all environmental laws and regulations. This accreditation underscores our firm commitment to environmental responsibility, including the proper handling of all waste through environmentally friendly procedures.
